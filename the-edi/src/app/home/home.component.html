EDI
Ethical Data Initiative
The EDI is a form of app observatory. It allows you to gain insight into what happens to your personal information when using mobile applications. Produced as part of a joint venture investigating the theory and practice of Social Machines.
A Social Machine is defined as a system comprised of both machine and human elements. Where social contributions made by the user enhances the service provided by the machine.[1] Information repositories like Wikipedia and social networks like Facebook are examples of social machines.
With approximately 2.8 million apps available in the google play store,[2] Many Social Machines are either entirely based around a mobile application or have an equivalent mobile form. Yet there remains little transparency with regards to what is being done with a user's personal information.
